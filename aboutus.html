About Me

Kelvin Nunez

Hi! My name is Kelvin Nunez and I am a web developer from NY. I started my web development journey back in July. Web development is challenging, but it's like playing a video game.

Before becoming a web developer, I spent 8 years in car sales for major car dealer brands and 1 year selling payroll and human resource solutions for ADP. While working for ADP, I became interested in learning web developement because many small businesses that I would come accross don't have a website or an online presence.

Knowing that fact, I started building a business to get small businesses online. Plus, what I offer to small business is not only a website, but a service that is built around trust and communication.
